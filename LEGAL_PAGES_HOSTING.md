## What You Need to Do

### 1. Update Contact Information

**In `privacy-policy.html`, replace:**
- `privacy@ctfgame.com` with your actual email

**In `terms-of-service.html`, replace:**
- `support@ctfgame.com` with your actual email

### 2. Host the Files

You need these pages publicly accessible on the web. Here are your options:

#### **Option A: GitHub Pages (FREE, Easiest)**

1. Create a new GitHub repo: `ctf-game-website`
2. Upload both HTML files
3. Enable GitHub Pages in repo Settings ‚Üí Pages
4. Your URLs will be:
   - `https://yourusername.github.io/ctf-game-website/privacy-policy.html`
   - `https://yourusername.github.io/ctf-game-website/terms-of-service.html`

#### **Option B: Netlify (FREE, Fast)**

1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Drag and drop the two HTML files
3. Get instant URLs like:
   - `https://yourapp.netlify.app/privacy-policy.html`
   - `https://yourapp.netlify.app/terms-of-service.html`

#### **Option C: Vercel (FREE, Developer-friendly)**

1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Upload files
3. Get URLs instantly

#### **Option D: Your Own Domain**

If you have a domain (e.g., `ctfgame.com`), upload to:
- `https://ctfgame.com/privacy-policy.html`
- `https://ctfgame.com/terms-of-service.html`

### 3. Add URLs to App Store Submission

When submitting to App Store Connect, you'll need to provide:

1. **Privacy Policy URL** - Required field
2. **Terms of Service URL** - Optional but recommended

**Where to add:**
- App Store Connect ‚Üí Your App ‚Üí App Information
- Look for "Privacy Policy URL" field

### 4. (Optional) Link from Within App

You can add links to these pages in your app's:
- Profile ‚Üí Help & Support
- Settings ‚Üí Legal
- Registration screen (small "Terms" link)
