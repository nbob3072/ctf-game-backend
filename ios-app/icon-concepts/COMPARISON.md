# Quick Icon Comparison

## At a Glance

| Concept | Style | Best For | Vibe |
|---------|-------|----------|------|
| **1. Flag Pin** | GPS marker + flag | Location emphasis | Accessible, friendly |
| **2. Shield + Flag** | Shield emblem | Team competition | Heroic, strategic |
| **3. Crosshair** | Tactical targeting | Precision gameplay | Tactical, serious |
| **4. Three-Team Flags** | Geometric pinwheel | Team rivalry | Unique, energetic |

## Color Palettes

**Concept 1:** Cyan/Blue + Orange + Navy background  
**Concept 2:** Blue/Purple + Orange + Dark background  
**Concept 3:** Cyan + Orange/Red + Black background  
**Concept 4:** Blue + Orange + Purple + Black background

## Recognition Factor

**Most Familiar:** Concept 1 (similar to location apps)  
**Most Unique:** Concept 4 (no similar icons in App Store)  
**Most Professional:** Concept 3 (modern, sleek)  
**Most Playful:** Concept 2 (game-like shield)

## Marketing Angles

### Concept 1 - "The Explorer"
*"Your next adventure is on the map"*
- Appeals to: Pokemon GO players, casual gamers
- Suggests: Exploration, discovery, navigation

### Concept 2 - "The Warrior"
*"Defend your team, capture their flag"*
- Appeals to: Strategy gamers, team players
- Suggests: Team pride, territory, combat

### Concept 3 - "The Tactician"
*"Precision. Strategy. Victory."*
- Appeals to: Competitive gamers, tacticians
- Suggests: Skill-based, strategic depth

### Concept 4 - "The Rivalry"
*"Three teams. One battlefield. Endless glory."*
- Appeals to: All players (unique hook)
- Suggests: Multi-team dynamics, balance, competition

## App Store Visibility

**Stands Out Most:** Concept 3 (cyan on black) or Concept 4 (tri-color)  
**Blends In Most:** Concept 1 (common GPS pin pattern)  
**Premium Look:** Concept 3  
**Fun/Approachable:** Concept 1 or 2

## Final Recommendation

**If your game is...**

- **Like Pokemon GO** → Use Concept 1
- **Like Clash of Clans** → Use Concept 2
- **Like Valorant/Apex Legends** → Use Concept 3
- **Something totally new** → Use Concept 4

**Personal favorite:** Concept 4 - most distinctive and directly represents the 3-team mechanic that makes your game unique.
