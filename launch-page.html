Frequently Asked Questions

How does GPS gameplay work?

The app uses your phone's GPS to show flags on a map. Walk to a flag location in the real world, get within 30 meters, and hold the position to capture it. The closer you are, the faster you capture.

What devices are supported?

CTF works on iOS 14+ (iPhone 8 and newer) and Android 8+ devices with GPS and internet connectivity. A stable data connection is required for real-time gameplay.

Is the game free?

Yes! CTF is free to download and play. Optional in-app purchases allow you to boost capture speed, unlock cosmetic items, and support your faction.

Can I play anywhere?

At launch, CTF is available in NYC, Chicago, Austin, and San Francisco. More cities are coming soon based on player demand!

How do factions work?

Choose Titans (aggressive), Guardians (defensive), or Phantoms (stealth) when you start. Your faction choice affects gameplay bonuses and team leaderboards. You can't switch factions once chosen.

Is multiplayer real-time?

Yes! All captures, battles, and leaderboard updates happen in real-time. When you capture a flag, other players in your city see the change immediately.
